VersoLight

We make tailor-made starry sky panels custom made.
The panels have an aluminum surface, are powder coated on the surface with painted edges and are ideal for use in wet rooms and even outdoors.

Installation is uncomplicated and runs fast.

If you do not want a construction site at home or if there is no suspended ceiling, then our finished starry sky is just the thing for you with a realistic starry sky, indirect light, recessed lighting or LED spots.

Lighter compared to similar sized constructions

The installation height of a prefabricated panel can be only 40 mm depending
on the equipment. (in exceptional cases, in some places even from 20mm).

Base model

  • Dimmable
  • Changeable LED bulbs
  • No heat development
  • Silently
  • Rectangular form
  • Height of the basic construction panel is about 55mm
  • Panel color - white, surface - aluminum
  • Degree of protection surface - IP 64
  • Ready for connection to 230-240 V & tilde; 50-60 Hz
  • Starry sky with either white light or RGB color change
  • Approx. 150 stars / m² different "big" stars
  • Including radio remote control

Motive of the starry sky

In the basic version we offer chaotically distributed stars of different sizes and possibly some zodiac signs. Other starry sky motifs need a significantly higher number of stars. We are happy to offer you

  • Starry sky at the time of your birth,
  • Star spiral or milky way with thousands of stars
  • We like to design a starry sky individually for you
  • Southern starry sky
  • Certain pictures etc
  • Northern starry sky

Indirect lighting & LED recessed spotlights and lights

If you tell us your approximate idea and send the layout of the room, we will make you an offer with a design proposal. If you like we will send you the exact drawing of the panel for release.

The heights of the construction panels can be selected as desired from 20mm and do not necessarily have to be the same (eg for the compensation of sloping roofs)

Colors & Dimensions

Most starlight panels are white. The color of the panel is important only during the day and should be appropriate to the room. In the dark, each panel also looks dark or black and you only see the stars.

Shapes, height

The shape of the panel can be individually designed. Almost every shape is possible.

If you tell us your approximate idea and send the layout of the room, we will make you an offer with a design proposal. If you like we will send you the exact drawing of the panel for release.

The heights of the construction panels can be selected as desired from 20mm and do not necessarily have to be the same (eg for the compensation of sloping roofs)

Multi-part panels

For larger rooms, we offer multi-part panels that adjoin one another.

Scope of application

Our panels are suitable for indoor and outdoor use, eg shower or outdoor.

The outdoor area is limited to covered ceilings such as balconies.

The max. possible ambient temperature is 40C °, for saunas up to max. 100C ° we offer suitable wood panels for this purpose.
