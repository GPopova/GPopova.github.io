The Law Offices of Proetta & Oliver, LLC

Attorney William A. Proetta is a seasoned criminal defense attorney and partner at Law Offices of Proetta & Oliver which practices criminal, DWI and municipal defense in Ocean County, New Jersey. We provide our full attention and focus to clients facing a wide range of disorderly persons offenses, indictable charges, and motor vehicle offenses. Led by attorney William Proetta, our Ocean County criminal defense practice offers clients the experience and poise typical at big firms, but with the individual client service of a smaller office.

We offer free initial consultations. Schedule an appointment to speak with an experienced lawyer about your case. Call (848) 238-2100 or fill out the online form below.

Our phones are answered 24 hours a day. We are available weekdays during business hours. We also meet with clients on evenings and weekends by request. We have multiple office locations conveniently located in Toms River, Middletown, Edison, Jersey City, and Cranford. All major credit cards are accepted.
